Transfer patient to your site

Transfer to site

After adding the patient the database it is not "attached" to any particular place or site.  You will need to attach him/her to your site before registering any data.  If you added the patient using the right-click method outline above, you are immediately asked to confirm that you want to transfer the patient to your location

 

Answer "Yes" to this question of it is the indeed a person you want to include in Ultradian.

 

Figure 2: Transferring a patient to your site.

 

After a patient is transferred to a site, he/she will be added the the list of patients visible at startup, but only at your particular site.  A user may be given privileges to move between sites using the "ChangeWorksite" role.  Ask the superuser for more information.
